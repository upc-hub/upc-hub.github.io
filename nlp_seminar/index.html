Asahi Laboratory - NLP Seminar

About the Seminar

This hands-on seminar, organized by the Asahi Laboratory at Nagoya University, introduces students to the fundamentals and applications of Natural Language Processing (NLP) using Large Language Models (LLMs). Participants will engage with interactive notebooks and real-world datasets to explore various NLP tasks.
