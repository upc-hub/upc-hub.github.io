Choosing Your Environment: Google Colab or JupyterHub

Google Colab

Google Colab provides a preconfigured environment running on a virtual machine. The connection may be automatically disconnected after a period of inactivity, requiring you to reconnect. If additional libraries or dependencies are needed, they must be reinstalled each time the connection is reestablished. If the required libraries are small, the installation time is usually acceptable. However, installing large dependencies repeatedly can be time-consuming and frustrating.

Requirements:

  • A Google account is required.

JupyterHub

JupyterHub provides a preconfigured environment hosted on our local server. Access to the university VPN is required, so it is not available to participants who are not affiliated with Nagoya University.

Requirements:

  • Access to the Nagoya University VPN
